Leafy Sea Dragon

The Leafy Sea Dragon is a fascinating example of evolutionary adaptation, with its intricate, leaf-like appendages designed for perfect camouflage in its seaweed-rich habitat. Despite its delicate appearance, it is a skilled swimmer, using its dorsal fin for propulsion while drifting gracefully through the water.

  • It is a close relative of the seahorse, found in the coastal waters of southern Australia.
  • Known for its impressive camouflage, it blends seamlessly with seaweed and kelp in its environment
  • This species is protected due to its habitat loss and pollution, there are estimated to be only a few thousand left!
